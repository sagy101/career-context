Use this registry when creating or updating final resume review prompts. Prefer newer dated sources when a target role, geography, company, or job posting changes. Access dates below reflect the source check performed during the 2026-05-04 implementation.

## Resume, ATS, And Market Guidance

- Use the newest relevant market scan under `guidance/market-scans/` for target-role requirements, recruiter keywords, and current skill expectations.
- Use `guidance/resume-best-practices.md` and `guidance/ats-and-format-checklist.md` for repo-local resume structure and artifact guidance.
- When updating those guidance files, prefer reputable university career centers, official job postings from target companies, and official technology documentation. Record URLs and access dates in the updated guidance file or market scan.
