# ATS And Format Checklist

- One page by default.
- Calibri or Arial.
- Conservative accent colors only.
- No text boxes, columns that break parsing, icons, charts, or layout tricks.
- Real hyperlinks, not raw long URLs in the rendered resume.
- PDF text extraction must be readable.
- Confirm PDF page count.
- Keep enough bottom whitespace.
- Avoid dense keyword blocks; use skills grouped by domain.
- Use standard section names such as Summary, Skills, Work Experience, Projects, Education, Certifications, Publications, Military Service, Volunteer Work, and Languages when relevant.
