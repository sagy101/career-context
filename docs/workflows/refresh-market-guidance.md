# Refresh Market Guidance Workflow

Use this before major positioning, skills, or ATS changes.

1. Confirm the target role family.
2. Search current trusted sources.
3. Prefer official job descriptions and reputable university career guidance.
4. Treat generic ATS blogs as secondary and lower trust.
5. Capture the scan date, sources, recurring skill clusters, recurring responsibilities, and resume implications.
6. Create a new dated scan under `guidance/market-scans/`; do not overwrite old scans.
7. Do not keyword-stuff the resume; use natural, evidence-backed wording.

## Freshness Rules

During final resume review, refresh or create a scan automatically when:

- no scan exists for the target role family
- the latest AI / agentic / ML infrastructure scan is older than 45 days
- the latest general full-stack / platform scan is older than 90 days
- the latest resume-format / ATS fundamentals scan is older than 180 days
- target role family, seniority, geography, company, or job description changed
- the candidate provides a specific job posting
